Blue Ridge Parkway

  • Borders both the Great Smoky Mountains and Shenandoah National Park.
  • Offers stunning views of Appalachia.
  • Stretches 469 miles along the crest of the Blue Ridge Mountains through North Carolina and Virginia.
  • Fall leaf peeping is a hugely popular activity along the Parkway, as are hiking and wildlife viewing.

Gettysburg National Military Park

  • Site of the largest battle ever waged during the American Civil War.
  • Where Lincoln delivered the Gettysburg Address.
  • Located 50 miles northwest of Baltimore, Maryland.
  • Its' cemetery contains more than 7,000 interments, including over 3,500 from the Civil War.

Fort McHenry National Monument and Historic Shrine

  • Francis Scott Key wrote "The Star-Spangled Banner" here during the Battle of Baltimore, September 13-14, 1814.
  • During the War of 1812, defenders of Fort McHenry stopped the British advance on Baltimore and helped to preserve the United States.
  • Remained an active military post, off and on, for nearly 100 years after the War of 1812.
  • Popular tourist attraction in the Baltimore, MD area.
